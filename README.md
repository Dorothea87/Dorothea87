### 👩‍💻 About me

I'm a software developer with a background in teaching and hospitality management — a combination that makes me unusually focused on the *why* behind code, not just the *how*.

I've spent the past year building production systems for HMRC using Scala and Play Framework, working across microservices, API integrations, and complex data flows. I care about writing code that is readable and well-documented, and I bring strong communication instincts to every team I work in.

Before tech I spent years explaining complex things to people who didn't want to hear them — first as a teacher, then as a manager. Turns out that's surprisingly useful in software.
